Welcome to Bitovi University. Learn front-end development, design, and management in these free courses!

Purpose

Bitovi is a front-end consulting company obsessed with making technology delivery better and sharing that knowledge with its clients and the community. To excel at technology delivery, there is an unlimited set of things you need to know.

Bitovi University is our attempt to create high-quality trainings for the most important concepts in front-end technology delivery.

Get Help

If you need help with an exercise, please reach out to us on gitter. If you'd like us (Bitovi) to teach you and your organization this material, you can hire us at https://www.bitovi.com/training.

Overview

Advanced JavaScript

DOM and jQuery

RxJS
